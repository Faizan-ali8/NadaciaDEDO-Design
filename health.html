Integrated health services

In extreme poverty, access to healthcare is often unavailable. That is why we are creating a model of integrated health-social support – we provide treatment and care, accompany clients to specialists, provide psychological assistance and remove practical barriers such as fees or bureaucracy.

A general practitioner for adults, a psychiatrist, a psychologist, and trained medical staff provide healthcare to vulnerable groups of the population as part of a multidisciplinary team, both in outpatient clinics and in the field, wherever homeless people live. We are bringing a unique comprehensive care model in Central Europe to Košice and, from 2026, to Prešov.
