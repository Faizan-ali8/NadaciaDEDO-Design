The Dual Pillar Framework

"Tailoring Workshop, as a social enterprise, where unique fashion bags and accessories are created from unnecessary banners, and the Supported Employment Agency - APZ are 2 basic pillars of work inclusion."

Operational Unit

Tailoring Workshop creates jobs and a fair, safe working environment. By transforming waste into value, we prove that systemic redundancy (both materials and people) is a design flaw, not an inherent trait.

Strategic Agency

APZ seeks opportunities on the open labor market. Professional job coaches work with clients individually, applying the Individual Placement and Support - IPS approach.

IPS Methodology

The basic principles of which help people regain self-worth and fulfill their career ambitions. We don't just "find jobs"; we match human capability to market demand with clinical precision.
