Methodology

Our field workers utilize the Comprehensive Field Social Services protocol—a rigorous, evidence-based framework that measures 14 distinct indicators of family stability every month.

Systemic intervention, not just aid.

The "Comprehensive field social services" provided by Nadasia Dedto are not a temporary fix. They represent a fundamental shift in how social engineering addresses homelessness. By deploying professional social workers directly into the field, we remove the barriers to entry that often prevent the most vulnerable from seeking help.

Crisis Intervention Immediate stabilization of households facing eviction or medical emergencies.
Long-term Mentoring Extended support structures focusing on financial literacy and domestic stability.

Our teams operate with a precision-engineered approach, ensuring that every hour of counseling is tracked against clear, measurable outcomes. This "Living Ledger" approach allows us to pivot resources to where they are most effective, maximizing impact for every family involved.
