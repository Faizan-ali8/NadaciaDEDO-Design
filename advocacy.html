Advocacy and research

We work closely with cities, public institutions, and European networks to create systemic changes in housing policy. Our research-driven approach ensures that advocacy is backed by robust data, transforming the way homelessness is addressed at both legislative and societal levels.

hub The PUB Platform

The Platform for Urban Betterment (PUB) serves as our central hub for collaborative research. By aggregating real-time data from local municipal departments and social providers, we create a unified ledger of needs and available resources. This platform eliminates administrative silos and allows for precision-targeted advocacy.

language FEANTSA Network

As an active member of FEANTSA (European Federation of National Organisations Working with the Homeless), Nadácia Dedo contributes local Slovakian insights to the European-wide database. This participation ensures our advocacy aligns with international best practices and contributes to the broader Continental strategy for ending homelessness.
